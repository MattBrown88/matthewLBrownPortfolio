The Challenge

The client ran on a wide range of tools for data visualization and analysis. While each tool had its specific use, they collectively lacked the versatility of an all-in-one, self-service analytics solution.

Maintenance of disparate dashboards was highly time-consuming.

Analysts routinely wrote SQL to extract data for ad-hoc reports.

No standard methods for retrieving cohort data.

Lack of a reliable, intuitive dashboard to access trusted data with ease.

The SolutionA Self-Service Analytics Tool

We designed a robust, self-service analytical tool with integrated data services. This solution empowers business analysts to test hypotheses, extract data, and generate insights — saving time and amplifying their ability to impact positive health outcomes.

Easy-to-use interface to explore data from multiple angles.

Extracts raw and aggregated data quickly and reliably.

Compares cohorts and tracks changes over time.

Supports data-driven decisions to close gaps in care and optimize programs.

Data Warehouse

Self-Service App

Real-time cohort exploration for business users — no SQL required.

Pre-aggregated datasets and a caching layer keep dashboards fast while holding down BigQuery costs — putting trusted data directly in the hands of business users who'd never had self-service access before.

Results

Reduced Analyst Effort

Business users can answer questions without relying on technical teams.

Faster Opportunity Assessment

Analysts can test hypotheses and evaluate member cohorts in minutes instead of days.

Better Clinical Decision Making

Enables identification of care gaps, intervention opportunities, and program optimization.

Enterprise Scale Analytics

Processes terabytes of healthcare data across 4 million members with a secure, intuitive self-service experience.

System Features

Filter & Explore

Filter down to your population or cohort of interest using 500+ indicators (e.g., benefits, conditions, clinical program enrollment, care gaps, etc.).

Compare & Track

Compare cohorts and track changes in group metrics over time to measure the effectiveness of interventions and identify trends.

Summarize & Visualize

View cohort-specific summaries and trends using a wide range of metrics and KPIs—such as cost, utilization, ratios, counts, prevalence, risk scores, and benchmarks.

Import & Export

Import and export member lists and raw data for deeper analysis and easy integration with other tools and workflows.
