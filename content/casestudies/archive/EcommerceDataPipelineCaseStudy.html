The Challenge

The client ran several Shopify storefronts with data scattered across Shopify, GA4, and Google Ads. With no central environment for cross-store analytics, each platform reported its own numbers, and reporting depended on manual exports, spreadsheets, and cleanup.

Fragmented Data

Data spread across Shopify, GA4, and Google Ads with no shared home.

No Centralized Reporting

No single environment for cross-store analytics.

Inconsistent Metrics

Different platforms produced different numbers.

Manual Reconciliation

Reporting relied on exports, spreadsheets, and manual cleanup.

Multi-Store Complexity

Difficult to analyze performance across multiple storefronts.

The Solution

Automated Data Ingestion

Airbyte automatically ingests data from Shopify, GA4, and Google Ads into the warehouse, replacing manual exports with scheduled, repeatable pipelines.

Centralized Data Warehouse

Consolidated every source into BigQuery as a single, governed home for all storefront data.

Data Modeling & Transformation

Modeled and transformed raw data with dbt into clean, tested tables with standardized business logic.

Self-Service Reporting

Exposed governed business metrics through a Lightdash semantic layer, with interactive dashboards and visualizations for every storefront.
